## Overview

DaisyDisk Professional Suite is a complete storage management and analysis platform that serves users who want a beautiful, fast, and thorough understanding of every byte on their drives before making any cleanup decisions.

Background scanning processes multiple volumes simultaneously in the background without blocking the interface, making it practical to analyze all connected drives and quickly compare space usage across storage devices.

Hidden space detection identifies system caches, iOS device backups, mail attachments, and other hidden storage consumers that Finder never shows, surfacing the complete picture of what is actually using your space.

Admin scanning mode accesses system-protected folders and files that are not visible to regular user scans, providing the most complete picture of storage usage available for users who want to understand every aspect of their system's disk utilization.

DaisyDisk Professional Suite is the disk analysis tool for users who appreciate the combination of beautiful design and genuine analytical depth, providing storage insight that is both visually compelling and practically useful for making informed cleanup decisions.

## Performance & Integration

DaisyDisk is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while DaisyDisk processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
